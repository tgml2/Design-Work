# Summary

This is the infinity cube from the SGI logo. The cubes can be printed together to make a mesh or chainmail (I've included examples of the arrangement). It is printed on a corner so that it can be printed without supports or assembly.

# Print Settings

Printer: Ultibots D300VS
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: .2 mm
Infill: 33% rectilinear 
Filament_brand: Ultibots
Filament_color: lime yellow
Filament_material: ABS

Notes: 
I printed the smaller cubes without brims. I wasn't brave enough to print the big one without, but I suspect it would work.
